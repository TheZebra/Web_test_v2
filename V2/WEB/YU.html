    What's Year Up?

    Recognizing there are over 12 million jobs available that are lacking the talent and 6 million young adults with talent that without opportunity; Year Up is a program dedicated to closing that Opportunity Divide. By identifying talent and training them for a year in total with 6 months in training and 6 months in internship applying the training.

    During the learning and development, trainees are taught in three different modules in which we learn: Hardware, software and for the third module we are put into tracks to reflect a specific field.
